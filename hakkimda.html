TED

TED Kayseri College Campus is a modern education campus built on a land area of ​​19,158.7 m2, with a closed area of ​​28,654 m2 and a landscape area of ​​12,400 m2. The campus includes a primary school building with a capacity of 600 students, a secondary school building with a capacity of 480 students and a high school building with a capacity of 696 students. In addition, individuals who will shape the world of the future are raised with the indoor sports complex, dining hall, outdoor playgrounds, laboratories, conference hall, club rooms and libraries. All classrooms are equipped with a computer, projector and internet connection.

sport center

In our sports center; • Semi-olympic swimming pool, • Ballet hall, • Fitness center, • Archery field • Basketball Court with 300 spectator capacity • Basketball Court Without Tribune • Small Basketball Court • Modern equipped changing rooms • Instructor rooms on each floor • Team meeting rooms • Infirmary • There is a cafeteria.

security

At TED Kayseri College, school security is provided by our day and night security personnel. In order to protect the life and property of students and employees, security guards are on duty 24 hours a day. The cameras placed inside and outside the entire school building work uninterruptedly and are monitored by the security unit. Entry and exit to the school are made under the control of security guards.

workshops

In TED Schools, our educational philosophy, which aims to prepare our students for their academic development as well as to raise them as physically, mentally and spiritually balanced and healthy individuals, is to prepare them for life as social, artistic, problem-solving, self-confident, creative and productive individuals. In this context, art, sports and social activities; themes such as environmental and health awareness are applied by associating them with the curriculum. By supporting all kinds of activities that will reveal and develop students' interests and talents, our school provides the opportunity to develop our students in every field and exhibit their products. There are many educational areas offered to our students in our school.
